## Core Features Overview

### 1. AI-Enhanced Claim Wizard Implementation

The ClaimGuru claim wizard incorporates the most advanced AI features in the industry:

#### **Step 1: Policy Upload & AI-Powered Auto-Population**
- **Document Processing**: Supports both full policies and declarations pages
- **AI Data Extraction**: Automatically extracts key policy information including:
  - Policy numbers and dates
  - Coverage types and limits
  - Deductibles and requirements
  - Covered territory and restrictions
- **Validation Engine**: Real-time validation of extracted data with confidence scoring
- **Auto-Population**: Automatically fills subsequent form fields based on extracted data
- **Advanced Analysis**: 
  - Fraud detection scoring
  - Compliance verification
  - Quality assessment metrics
  - Weather correlation analysis

#### **Step 2-3: Client & Address Details with AI Verification**
- **Smart Validation**: Cross-references client information with policy data
- **Address Intelligence**: Google Places API integration with territory validation
- **Co-Insured Detection**: AI suggests co-insured parties based on policy analysis
- **Real-time Alerts**: Immediate flagging of inconsistencies or potential issues
- **Geographic Risk Assessment**: Evaluates location-based risk factors

#### **Step 4-6: Insurance & Coverage with AI Cross-Reference**
- **Policy Validation**: Verifies policy numbers against uploaded documents
- **Date Intelligence**: Validates loss dates against policy periods
- **Coverage Suggestions**: AI recommends appropriate coverage types based on policy
- **Deductible Verification**: Cross-checks deductibles with policy terms
- **Duplicate Detection**: Identifies potential duplicate payments
- **Limit Validation**: Ensures coverage limits don't exceed policy maximums
