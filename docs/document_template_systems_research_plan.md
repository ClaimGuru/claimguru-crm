# Research Plan: State-of-the-art Document Template Generators and Systems in 2025

## Objectives
- Analyze the current landscape of document template generation systems.
- Identify strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for innovation in existing solutions.
- Provide a detailed report on the findings.

## Research Breakdown
1.  **Enterprise Solutions:**
    *   PandaDoc
    *   DocuSign
    *   HelloSign
    *   Adobe Sign
    *   Microsoft Word templates
    *   Google Docs templates

2.  **Developer-Focused Solutions:**
    *   React-PDF
    *   jsPDF
    *   PDFKit
    *   Puppeteer for PDF generation
    *   HTML-to-PDF solutions

3.  **Legal/Insurance Industry Solutions:**
    *   HotDocs
    *   Contract Express
    *   Smokeball
    *   Other legal document automation tools

4.  **Modern SaaS Solutions:**
    *   Notion templates
    *   Coda templates
    *   Airtable templates

## Key Research Areas
*   Token/Variable Systems
*   Template Editors (UX)
*   Conditional Logic
*   Integration Capabilities
*   Output Formats
*   Workflow Integration
*   User Experience

## Specific Focus Areas for Insurance/Legal
*   Contract generation with complex clauses
*   Dynamic fee calculations and splitting
*   Multi-party document generation
*   Signature workflows and approval processes
*   Template versioning and compliance tracking

## Innovation Opportunities
*   AI-powered template suggestions
*   Smart field detection and mapping
*   Real-time collaboration on templates
*   Advanced formatting and styling capabilities
*   Mobile-optimized template creation

## Resource Strategy
- **Primary data sources:** Web search for official product websites, documentation, and reviews.
- **Search strategies:** Use targeted keywords for each system and research area. For example: "PandaDoc conditional logic", "React-PDF vs jsPDF", "HotDocs insurance contract generation".

## Verification Plan
- **Source requirements:** Triangulate information from at least 3 independent sources for each key finding. Prioritize official documentation, industry reports, and reputable tech review sites.
- **Cross-validation:** Compare feature descriptions and user reviews across multiple platforms to identify inconsistencies.

## Expected Deliverables
- A comprehensive research report in Markdown format, addressing all the user's requirements.
- The report will be saved to `docs/document_template_systems_research_2025.md`.

## Workflow Selection
- **Primary focus:** Search-focused. I will start by gathering a broad range of information on the listed systems and research areas. After initial information gathering, I will move to a verification-focused approach for key features and comparisons.
