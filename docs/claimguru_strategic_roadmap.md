### Market Gaps Identified:
1. **No true AI-first comprehensive CRM** - Brelly.ai has AI but lacks CRM depth
2. **Limited mobile optimization** - Most solutions are web-first
3. **Inflexible pricing models** - Single-tier or unclear pricing
4. **Weak integration ecosystems** - Limited third-party connectivity
5. **Basic reporting and analytics** - No predictive insights
